Feature Mapping for RTL:
========================
RTL mem_feature_idx [1:0] | Original Feature | Description
---------------------------|------------------|------------------
2'b00                      | 0x00             | Timestamp
2'b01                      | 0x01             | Arbitration ID
2'b10                      | 0x10             | Data Field
2'b11                      | 0xFF (leaf)      | Leaf Node
